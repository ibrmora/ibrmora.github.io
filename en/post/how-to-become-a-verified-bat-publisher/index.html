How to become a verified BAT publisher

23/05/2018

Ads and tracking software fill the web, which is an important factor in the bad experience of surfing the web sites and constitute a violation of privacy through the continuous tracking of users. That’s why browser extensions that block these ads have spread with varying efficiencies and effectiveness in performing that task.

On the other hand, publishers need a business model that can finance their publishing platforms and ads are the main, if not the only source of revenue. The inventor of JavaScript, Brendan Eich, proposed a solution to the dilemmas. Beginning with the launch of an open-source browser brave. Brave’s main task is to preserve the privacy of the user by blocking the tracking software and provide a wonderful user experience blocking ads effectively.

And then he designed the Ledger system that calculates user attention, and also payment system Basic Attention Token for publishers that allows the user to easily support his favorite content. This program is built into the brave browser and is available as an add-on for other browsers https://www.batify.net.

To benefit as a content publisher from the above system you need to verify your website address at https://publishers.basicattentiontoken.org, please follow the explanation below to be able to do so.

To start the authentication process click GET STARTED button and enter your email address

Your access link will be sent to your email address

Enter your name to complete the registration process

If you want to sign in with two-factor authentication do that option

Once signed in, you’ll be directed to the home page of your account

Click the Add channel button at the top of the page and then choose WEBSITE card

Enter your web address without prefix ibrmo.com

Choose Edit DNS records verification method

Copy the Information box for the Value record

Do not click the Verify DNS record button until you have finished adding the record to your website address

Create a corresponding record of the data below in your domain address settings with your DNS provider

Record Type Host Value TTL
TXT @ brave-ledger-verification=eaa906fc3f
dc1eb317942d71ac0605dac3a10ae2
1a4f4520591a67d1d2034ec1		 Automatic

In the command line, make sure that the record is published in the Web

$ nslookup -q=TXT ibrmo.com

Server:		127.0.1.1
Address:	127.0.1.1#53

Non-authoritative answer:
ibrmo.com	text = "v=spf1 include:spf.efwd.registrar-servers.com ~all"
ibrmo.com	text = "brave-ledger-verification=eaa906fc3fdc1eb317942d71ac0605dac3a10ae21a4f4520591a67d1d2034ec1"
